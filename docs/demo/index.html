axm-sfn · custody demo
Proof of what a machine made.

This turns an ordinary 3D printer into a node that can prove what it produced. Every second of a print is sealed into a tamper-evident chain — so the record can't be quietly edited after the fact without it showing.

1 Press ▶ Play — watch the custody chain build one signed packet per second as the printer heats, prints, and finishes.
2 Try the scenarios — a thermal excursion trips the policy verdict; a provenance fault is the printer going silent while the clock keeps recording.
3 Hit ⚠ Tamper after a clean run — forge a single packet and watch the chain break exactly where it was touched.
Reopen anytime with the ? button.
